Banner

A banner displays a prominent message at the top of the screen.

Warning

The default form of a banner. Use warning banners when you want the user to take a specific action or to warn them that something is about to go wrong.

Warning goes here

Use error banners to inform users something critical has happened and requires immediate attention.

Announcement

Announcement banners are used by admins who want to make a general announcement about the product. These banners do not contain an icon. Lengthy text will overflow, allowing users to scroll to see the rest of the content.

Truncation

Banner size can change based on the size of the browser. For warning and error banners, lengthy text will be truncated with an ellipses.
